Common Interview Questions

Prepare your answers to these frequently asked questions:

  1. Tell me about yourself.
  2. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
  3. Why do you want to work at our company?
  4. Can you describe a time when you overcame a significant challenge?
  5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  6. How do you handle feedback or criticism?
  7. What makes you the best candidate for this position?
  8. Can you explain this gap in your resume?
